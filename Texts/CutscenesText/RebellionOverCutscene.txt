A quiet thump. A bloody rose on the crumbled stones of the cave. This was the last one of them.

Deafening silence now filling the place, the Knight finds himself... pondering.

A few moments ago, he slaughtered three floors of the rebellion’s elders – all wise and respectful men.

Was that necessary at all? However, the king’s order was particularly precise.

The rebels have been killing the country’s townsfolk, spreading the all-consuming flames of war everywhere their eye could see. Nevertheless…

Everything he could think of has been the spread of violence and hatred.

He has seen a lot on his journey, oft meeting dangerous outlaws who have gone astray due to the chaos around them.

He had to talk to the king.
